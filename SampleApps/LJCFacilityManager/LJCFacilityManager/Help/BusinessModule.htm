Business Module

A Business can be a business that owns the facility, units or fixtures. It could also be a business that is contracted for repairs or maintenance.
Information can be managed for entities related to the business such as contacts and addresses.

Contact

This related item allows business contacts to be defined for the business.

Address

This related item records the addresses for the defined business.
