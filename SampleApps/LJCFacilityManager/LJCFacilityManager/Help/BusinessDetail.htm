Business Detail Dialog

The Business Detail dialog allows the user to create and edit business entries.

Form Controls

Name

This is the business name.

Description

This is the business description.

Business Type

This is the selected business type.
Only code types that belong to the Business code type class will appear in this list.

Effective Date

This is the date that the business is available for use. You can type the date into the date mask field or select the button to the right of the field to display the date selection dialog.

Expiration Date

This is the date after which the business is no longer available for use. You can type the date into the date mask field or select the button to the right of the field to display the date selection dialog.

Phone

This is the business phone number.

Extension

This is the phone extension.

Fax

This is the business fax number.

OK Button

Saves the entered data and exits the dialog.

Cancel Button

Exits the dialog without saving the entered data.
