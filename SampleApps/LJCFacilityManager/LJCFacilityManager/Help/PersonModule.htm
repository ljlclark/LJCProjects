Person Module

A Person is an individual associated with the facility or unit. It can be the current occupant of a hotel room, students in a classroom, employees or other associated individuals.
Information can be managed for entities related to the individual such as relationships, addresses, units to which the individual is assigned and accounts owned by the individual.

Relation

This related item allows relationships to be defined between the defined individuals.

Address

This related item records the addresses for the defined individual.

Unit

This item records the units occupied or assigned to an individual.

Account

This item records accounts owned by the individual.
