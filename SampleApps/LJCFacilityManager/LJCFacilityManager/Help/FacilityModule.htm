Facility Module

A Facility is usually a building that contains multiple rooms or units. This can be a Hotel/Motel, School, Business or other similar entity.
Information can be managed for entities related to the facility such as the contained units, the fixtures in those units and the equipment used to repair, maintain or temporarily enhance those units.

Unit

A Unit is usually a room in a facility. It could however represent bins or other storage units.

Fixture

A Fixture is an item that is usually not removed from the unit. Examples of fixtures can be Air Conditioners, Refrigerators, Microwave ovens, Desks, lamps and other furniture.

Equipment

Equipment usually represents something that is not permanently assigned to the unit. This can be audiovisual equipment or repair and maintenance equipment that is currently being used in that unit.
