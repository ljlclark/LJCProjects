Conversion Formula

A Conversion Formula is used to define a relationship between Data Items. It has the following format.

LeftItemName is # RightItemName

A Conversion Formula is identified by the "is" keyword in the second position.

The LeftItem will consist of a specific number of the RightItem.

Family is 5 Persons

The RightItem must always be a smaller part of the LeftItem.

Here are two additional Conversion Formulas: 

    Community is 16 Families
    Village is 4 Communities
